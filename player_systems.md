# 🧍 PLAYER SYSTEMS

## 🎯 Core Idea
The player is a normal human living in a modern city.  
No superpowers. No fantasy abilities. Only real-world survival, choices, and consequences.

---

## 1️⃣ Movement System
- Player can walk in all directions
- Sprinting uses stamina
- Stopping restores stamina
- Movement works on:
  - Keyboard (PC)
  - Touch joystick (mobile later)

---

## 2️⃣ Interaction System
- Player can interact with objects and people
- One button: “Interact”
- Examples:
  - Talk to NPC
  - Enter building
  - Pick up item
  - Start job task

---

## 3️⃣ Health System
- Player has health bar
- Health decreases from:
  - Fighting
  - Injury events
- Health restores by:
  - Resting
  - Medical help (later)

---

## 4️⃣ Money System
- Player starts with small amount of money
- Money is used for:
  - Food
  - Rent
  - Transport
- Money is earned through:
  - Jobs
  - Tasks
  - Side hustles

---

## 5️⃣ Time System
- Game runs in real-day cycle:
  - Morning
  - Afternoon
  - Evening
  - Night
- Some jobs only appear at certain times

---

## 6️⃣ Basic Needs (Future Expansion)
Not active yet, but planned:
- Hunger
- Sleep
- Stress

---

## 🧠 DESIGN RULE
Everything must stay simple first.  
No complex systems until core movement works perfectly.

---

## 🚫 IMPORTANT LIMITATION
If a system does not work in a basic prototype, it will NOT be added yet.
