# 🌍 WORLD SYSTEMS

## 🎯 Core Idea
The game world is a living modern city.  
It reacts to time, player actions, and NPC behavior.

The world is small at first, then expands over time.

---

## 🏙️ 1️⃣ City Structure (START SMALL)
The first version of the city includes:

- 1 small street
- 1 apartment building
- 1 shop
- 1 job location
- 1 open walkable area

No big map yet. Focus on density, not size.

---

## 🚶 2️⃣ NPC SYSTEM
NPCs are simple AI characters that:

- Walk around on paths
- Go to work and return home
- React to player actions
- Have basic dialogue lines

Each NPC has:
- Name
- Job
- Daily routine

---

## ⏰ 3️⃣ CITY TIME SYSTEM
The world changes based on time:

- Morning: people go to work
- Afternoon: active movement
- Evening: return home
- Night: low activity, higher risk events

Some shops and jobs close at night.

---

## 🏪 4️⃣ INTERACTIVE BUILDINGS
Buildings the player can enter:

- Apartment (safe zone, save/rest)
- Shop (buy items)
- Job place (earn money)

Each building has a simple interior scene.

---

## 🚓 5️⃣ CITY RESPONSE SYSTEM (BASIC)
The world reacts to actions:

- Fighting → NPCs run or call police
- Crime → reputation decreases
- Good actions → trust increases

Police system is simple at first:
- Appear after repeated crime
- Chase player
- Reset situation if caught

---

## 🌧️ 6️⃣ ENVIRONMENT EFFECTS (LIGHT)
Simple changes only:

- Day/night lighting
- Rain (visual only at first)
- Mood tone changes based on time

---

## 🧠 DESIGN RULE
The city must feel alive even if it is small.

Small world + smart behavior > big empty map.

---

## 🚫 IMPORTANT LIMITATION
Do NOT expand the map until all systems work correctly in one area.
