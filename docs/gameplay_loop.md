# 🔁 GAMEPLAY LOOP SYSTEM

## 🎯 Core Idea
The game is built around a repeating life cycle.

The player wakes up, makes choices, earns or loses resources, survives the day, and repeats.

There is no fixed storyline. The loop creates the story.

---

## 🧭 1️⃣ CORE DAILY LOOP

Every in-game day follows this structure:

### Morning
- Player wakes up
- Check money and health
- Decide what to do for the day

### Daytime
- Go to work OR explore city
- Earn money or take risks
- Interact with NPCs

### Evening
- Return home or roam city
- Spend money (food, transport, needs)
- Social interactions

### Night
- Rest or take risky actions
- Higher danger events
- Save progress (optional safe zone only)

---

## 💰 2️⃣ PLAYER DECISION LOOP

Every action leads to one of three outcomes:

- Gain money
- Lose money
- Change reputation

There are no neutral actions.

Everything has a consequence.

---

## ⚖️ 3️⃣ RISK SYSTEM

Player constantly chooses between:

- Safe actions (low reward, stable life)
- Risky actions (high reward, high danger)

Example:
- Job = safe money
- Street hustle = risky money
- Crime = very high risk/reward

---

## 🧠 4️⃣ PROGRESSION SYSTEM

Player does NOT level up in a traditional way.

Instead, progression is:

- Better jobs unlock
- Better areas unlock
- NPC respect increases
- Opportunities appear naturally

---

## 🔁 5️⃣ FAILURE LOOP

Failure is part of gameplay:

- Losing money does NOT end game
- Arrest sends player into time penalty
- Injury slows progression
- Bankruptcy forces lifestyle reset

The world continues.

---

## 🌍 6️⃣ GAME PHILOSOPHY

- No scripted story
- No forced missions
- No perfect path

The game is shaped by the player’s behavior.

---

## 🚫 IMPORTANT RULE

If an action does not affect at least one system:
- Money
- Time
- Health
- Reputation

Then it should NOT exist in the game.
